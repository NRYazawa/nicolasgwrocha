About Me

Software Developer & Independent Creator

Hi! I am a Software Developer specialized in object-oriented programming (C++, C#, Java) and the creation of interactive systems. As the founder of the independent studio The Akai Kitsune, I have experience managing the entire software lifecycle: from prototyping and system architecture to optimization and final deployment.

Through The Akai Kitsune, I also author critical essays and analytical reviews, leveraging my technical background to deconstruct game mechanics and software design choices.

My background includes developing complex mechanics (inventory logic, real-time AI, shader optimization) in Unreal Engine, as well as integrating software with physical hardware (IoT/Arduino). Beyond entertainment, I have also developed applications focused on digital literacy and accessibility, such as DigiAid, developing social media simulators in collaboration with psychology professionals.

I am a fast learner and a proactive developer, passionate about problem-solving and building robust, efficient systems.
