Penetras Bons de Papo

Analog Tabletop Game | Core Game Design & Balancing

Game Design Analog Mechanics Playtesting Social Dynamics

Project Overview

Penetras Bons de Papo is a complete analog social card game designed from the ground up to promote conversation, deception, and interaction. The premise is simple but highly engaging: players must identify the impostor among them. However, they are forced to constantly change their communication strategies based on dynamic "modification cards" drawn during the game.


Key Design Implementations

1. Core Loop & Rule Balancing

Developing an analog game strips away the luxury of programmed constraints, meaning the rules must be flawlessly balanced and intuitive. I focused on iterative design, ensuring that the "impostor" mechanics were fair and that the modification cards provided enough chaos to be fun without breaking the game's core logic.

2. Extensive Playtesting

The game underwent numerous rounds of blind playtesting to refine the social dynamics. Observing how different groups interpreted the rules and interacted allowed me to tighten the instructions and adjust the card abilities, teaching me invaluable lessons about player behavior and mechanics onboarding.
