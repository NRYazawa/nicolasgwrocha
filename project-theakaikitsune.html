Project Overview

The Akai Kitsune is a dedicated website and publishing platform created to host essays, articles, and deep-dive reviews. It serves as a centralized hub for sharing my insights on game design, mechanics, and industry trends. The goal was to establish a space for meaningful dialogue and reflection, connecting readers from various backgrounds—including gamers, developers, and enthusiasts.


Key Technical & Design Implementations

1. Platform Architecture & User Experience

Designed the website structure to prioritize readability and content discovery. The layout was optimized to handle long-form essays, ensuring that typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy keep the reader engaged without distractions.

2. Analytical Deconstruction

Beyond the technical setup of the site, the core of this project is the content itself. Writing these articles requires a deep understanding of software architecture, game loops, and player psychology, allowing me to deconstruct successful titles and apply those lessons to my own development pipeline.
